Resources

Welcome to my collection of research and learning resources. Over the years, I have come across numerous tools, tutorials, databases, lecture notes, and websites that have been invaluable in my development as a researcher in high-energy astrophysics and computational science. This page serves as a curated repository of resources that I personally find useful for students and researchers interested in astronomy, X-ray data analysis, machine learning, scientific programming, and graduate studies. The links provided here belong to their respective authors and organizations, and full credit goes to the original creators of the content. I plan to continuously update this page with additional material, tutorials, software guides, lecture notes, and opportunities that may be helpful to the broader scientific community. I hope you find these resources useful in your own academic and research journey.
